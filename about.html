Dasha Pruss

My name is Dasha and I'm a graduate student in the Information Science PhD program at the University of Colorado, Boulder. Information Science is a new department that's an interdisciplinary mix of computer science, the humanities, and the sciences. My research background is in computational biology and applied data science, and I'm interested in applying computational techniques like natural language processing and machine learning to address questions in epidemiology and the social sciences.

.

I graduated from the University of Utah in 2016 with a BS in Computer Science. I am an NSF fellow and have worked in research groups in biochemistry, computational genomics, and biological data visualization at the University of Utah and Harvard Medical School. I am also involved with STEM outreach and have spent two years as a computer science teaching assistant.
